Aeho

A Game about Atonement

About the Game

Aeho is a game about atonement. It is made by Eric Rawn, a high school student from Maine.

It is a 3D Fixed Axis Adventure game about a person (you), and your journey to find something. Life, enlightentment, atonement, inner peace, the player can decide.
